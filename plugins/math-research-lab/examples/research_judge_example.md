# Research Judge Example

```text
Use $research-judge.

Critically judge the claim:
"The observed improvement is caused by mechanism M."

Do not improve the writing.
Separate measurement, correlation, mechanism, theory, implementation, and speculation.
List the evidence that alternatives must preserve.
Find real alternative explanations.
Do not downgrade the claim without justification.
Rank decisive experiments.
Provide the strongest justified wording.
```
