# Downgrade Justification Protocol

Before downgrading any claim, answer:

1. Which exact claim is being downgraded?
2. What is the downgraded formulation?
3. Is the issue missing evidence, contradictory evidence, or artifact-risk?
4. Which critique-taxonomy category applies?
5. What evidence supports the downgrade?
6. What evidence still supports the original claim?
7. Which alternative explanation is at least equally consistent?
8. What smallest decisive test could restore or further weaken the claim?
9. Is the downgrade justified?

If these questions cannot be answered, do not downgrade.
Mark the claim as not fully evaluated.
