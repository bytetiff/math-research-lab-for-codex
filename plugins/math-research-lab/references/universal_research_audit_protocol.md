# Universal Research Audit Protocol

Default sequence:

1. Formalize the problem.
2. Audit notation and assumptions.
3. Verify the metric, derivation, or experimental result.
4. Identify artifacts and alternative explanations.
5. Apply relevant numerical, statistical, geometric, spectral, or optimization audits.
6. Only then mine candidate concepts.
7. Disambiguate concepts before naming contributions.
8. Store candidate concepts with evidence levels.
9. Run balanced research judgment.
10. Recommend the smallest decisive next experiment.

At every step, separate measurement from mechanism and supported scope from speculative scope. No unsupported overclaim. No unsupported downgrade.
